what is third place?

a ‘third place’ refers to the sociological idea that human beings are happier when they have a third place that is separate from home (first place) and work (second place). in the pandemic, a lot of us lost not only a third place, but our second places too. this site was created to encourage locals to go out and explore their city in the hope of finding a new third place. explore, discover, and connect with locals by sharing your favourite spots in the city. feel free to add a note to the map! or simply just browse others stories. thirdplace is from locals, for locals.
